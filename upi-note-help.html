How to Add Note in UPI Apps

Adding the Booking ID in the UPI payment note is crucial for your payment to be processed correctly. Below are examples showing where to enter the note in popular UPI apps:

Google Pay

Add the Booking ID in the "Note" section before confirming payment.

PhonePe

Locate the "Add Note" field and enter your Booking ID.

Paytm

Enter your Booking ID in the note section before proceeding.

QR not recognized or Note option missing?

Some apps may not allow adding a note if scanning a QR code. In such cases, please use manual payment (see last option on the QR page) to ensure your Booking ID is included and payment is valid.
